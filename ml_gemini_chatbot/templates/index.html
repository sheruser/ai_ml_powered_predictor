About this app

This application predicts if you're more of an introvert or extrovert based on your behavioral patterns. After prediction, Google's Gemini AI explains the results.

How it works

  1. Enter your behavioral information
  2. Click the analyze button
  3. Get your personality prediction and explanation
Understanding Personality

Introverts typically:

  • Prefer solitude and quiet environments
  • Feel drained after social interactions
  • Have deeper connections with fewer people

Extroverts typically:

  • Gain energy from socializing
  • Enjoy group activities and social events
  • Are often more outspoken and gregarious

Your inputs help our AI algorithm determine your personality tendencies.
